The Torrqe Motion Stack.

Torrqe integrates transmission architecture, advanced bearing elements, superelastic materials, and oil-free surfaces into compact motion systems for harsh, high-load environments.

Gear-Bearing Core

Combines gear reduction and bearing support into a compact transmission architecture for precise, load-capable motion.

Advanced Bearing Elements

High-performance alloy bearing elements designed for corrosion resistance, shock tolerance, and demanding mechanical environments.

Superelastic Motion Materials

High-strength superelastic materials for gears, bearing races, bushings, and actuator internals where wear, impact, and corrosion matter.

Oil-Free Surface System

Solid lubricant surfaces that reduce dependence on conventional oils in high-temperature, high-duty, and low-maintenance motion systems.

Integrated Motion Platform.

The same core architecture can be adapted across robot joints, UAV payloads, EV subsystems, and defense motion assemblies.
