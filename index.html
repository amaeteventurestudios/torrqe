The Motion Stack
Is the Bottleneck.

Robots, drones, vehicles, valves, and defense systems do not fail only at the motor. They fail at the motion layer, where control, gearing, bearings, materials, and surfaces have to work together under load, shock, heat, corrosion, and tight tolerances.

Today, much of that stack is fragmented across imported drives, custom reducers, standard bearings, oil-based lubrication, and supplier chains that were not built for rapid autonomous-machine production.

The Torrqe Motion Stack.

Torrqe integrates motor control, zero-backlash transmission architecture, advanced bearing elements, superelastic materials, and oil-free surfaces into compact motion systems for harsh, high-load environments.

01 Precision Motor Control

Low-speed BLDC control for smooth motion across wide speed ranges and precise output behavior.

02 Gear-Bearing Core

Combines gear reduction and bearing support into a compact transmission architecture for precise, load-capable motion.

03 Advanced Bearing Elements

High-performance NiTi-Hf bearing components designed for corrosion resistance, shock tolerance, and demanding mechanical environments.

04 Superelastic Motion Materials

60NiTi components for gears, races, bushings, and actuator internals where wear, impact, and corrosion matter.

05 Oil-Free Surface System

PS400 / PM400 solid lubricant surfaces that reduce friction, wear, and dependence on conventional oils in high-duty motion systems.
